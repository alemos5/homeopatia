Simmetry Dynamics

The manifestation Phases of the different simmetries, are under no doubt, dynamic, meaning not static.

It all depends on the individual´s interaction with its environment, regarding the different circumstances and their corresponding influence.

So much so, both the 2 Phases and their 4 Determinations, may vary from one circumstance to another.

At times one may be in harmony,  then undergo an excess or a shortage, facing different situations or eventualities.

Then the Stability Phase might imply harmony, where the individual ought to be at its peak, depending on their own singularities and individual talents.

When an individual gets stuck in any of the Instability Phase Determinations, he will then start losing integral harmony.  This lack of harmony will distort his perception of reality, as if  watching through a prism, thus originating a totally different way of feeling and interacting in the world.

People responding to symmetry order 1:

  • Are always active and are easily recognized because they never rest, that their necessary condition towards the world is movement.
  • They are constantly working, surrounding themselves by newcomers who they dazzle with their knowledge, intelligence, their brand-new projects and their wit.

    Reluctant to follow others, more inclined to be the ones who guide the steps to follow.
  • Many people see them as role-models and they ususally transmit energy and inspire trust upon their subordinates or their family and their close circles, given their tendency: coverage of the surroundings.
  • Nevertheless, their mineral heart will make them always seek for perfection and stability (necessary condition and individual desideratum), they might turn out to be highly demanding with themselves and others, never giving up if they can prove themselves better with a new action.
  • In harmony, the challenges they may actively pose to others, will be in most cases successful as they will rely upon their own identity traits (Theme: consolidation) (Experience: stability of their individuality)

Due to this articulation: animal towards the world and mineral in the heart:

  • They will seek to be appreciated for their individuality and personality, for those assets that make them unique and make them stand out from the rest.
  • Guided by this urge, they will search for spaces within a structure: meaning, they will need that all the spaces they cover {social activities, family, affections have to be perfectly ruled by a stable structure} They will order their actions by an inner classification thus imprinting reality with their personal seal.
  • That is why all his movements will always aim at his personal consolidation.
  • That is also why they will always try expansion in the world to reach perfection as a primordial goal in their lives.

  1. Relation to the world (Tendency): influenced by mineral information.
  2. Relation to himself, his Heart (Experience) is of vegetal nature.
  3. Inner nature (Rescue, Restoration) is of animal nature.

People responding to symmetry order 8 seek mainly to be accepted (Vital Need) }, that is their modus vivendi and what guides most of their acts; but, they wish to be accepted for their emotional abilities  (Patrimony) It´s in the way they feel and how they show it, that their inner world takes shape onto reality.

People responding to symmetry order 8:

  • may be recognized for their apparent discretion and simplicity, and their stability (Necessary condition to the world) and they are always working to guide their lives on works through which they will feel integrated (World Desideratum)to reality and might outlive them so as to leave a legacy of their sensitivity (Inner theme).
  • They are not very talkative with strangers, not very showy, often submerged in their own thoughts and ideas. They integrate to the group they belong to, that makes the structure of his life almost invariable (Tendencia).
  • AThey love it peace and quiet, in many cases almost absolutely. These people find in their sensitivity (Inner theme) refuge from the world. They enjoy sitting quietly reading a book, watching films on T.V. or they may spend the whole morning pondering about new plans and ideas.
  • Though they seem to live in a lonely world, once they gain confidence, they reveal themselves as really loving and honouring their own work, eager to improve their skills and profession, often getting perfectionist and obsessive about details; having a great ability to vary (Necessary inner condition)the emotional guide-line of their purposes within their life-structure.
  • These people do not ususally dream with long trips or to stand out in some sport, they do wish to keep on nourishing their intellect or improving their art-skills, to accomplish astonishing works that will make them part of to the communities or groups they aspire to belong to, because deep in their hearts they long to manifest their sensitivity (Individual Desideratum).
  • They may be great art-followers, in any of its expressions and usually show a refined and exquisite taste in every sphere of their lives. They are often that kind of person, with the prompt and good advise, usually surprising with their unique and useful remarks, witnessing they have not lived their lives in vain, as an intent for their own perpetuation (Experience) through these actions.
  • They usually don´t talk about what they don´t know and finding themselves surrounded by people they don´t hold many points in common, they usually withdraw and may get lost in their own thoughts. In fact, it is not unfrequent that they arise from this apparent absence, enticed by a new idea or the solution to a problem that had kept them worried.

        
Due to this articulation: mineral to the world and vegetal heart:

  • They will wish to be accepted for their sensitivity for their emotional abilities that make them unique and stand out from the rest.
  • Within that desire they will look for boundaries in their variability: meaning they will need to clearly define which is the limit for every circumstance in (social, family, emotional activities). They will put in order their actions and creativity, as if needing to know the right perspective of possibilities within reality.
  • That's why from this stability they will always try and express their inner sensibility.
  • That´s why they will look for integration in the world, to manifest their sensitivity as a primordial goal in their lives.
  • Therefore this tendency to move within the structure invariability implies the possibility of expressing their sensitivity& projecting to the world the Non conscious purpose of their Animal Nature: manifesting the Versatility of expansion.

Instability Phase
 (inharmonic)

Instability by Excess

Relation to the world (Tendency): Resistance to the environment. Inclusion. Rejection.
Relation to himself (Experience): Circumstancial Adjustment. Justification

When a person reponding to Symmetry order 8 suffers an Instability Phase by Excess:

  • It will enhance some of the mineral traits in such a way, that in spite of numerous intents, they will never manage to fully integrate the environment nor to others, turning their rich interior universe into a disorganized chaos never managing to fullfil it in a complete work. It´s the case of those writers who cannot mange to grasp their great potential into a story or a novel, in spite of all the ideas flourishing in their heads. Not being able to produce or to give shape in real life to their wishes, they will appeal to justification for their acts or will adjust depending on the circumstances.
  • In such a way, his response to the environment will always present a polarity: rejection or forced adjustment. Thus, his heart will determine an adecuación which will only last circumstancially and his relation to the environment will be experienced painfully.

Instability Phase
 (inharmonic)

Instability by Shortage

Relation to the world (Tendency): Contraction
Relation to himself (Experience): Fear with hesitation.

When a person responding to symmetry order 8 suffers an Instability Phase by Shortage:

  • life will find him enclosed within himself, totally contracted into his own boundaries and without self-confidence. They may turn into hesitant, persons, who seem to have lost their inspiration and the north that guided their lives. In meetings, they seem absent but not because their thoughts are elsewhere but because they have been caught by melancholy for no apparent reason. A deep hesitation has appeared, leaving them helpless.

When a person who responds to symmetry 8 suffers an Instability Phase by Shortage the following manifests itself:

  • Inner Nature/restoration/rescue:               

Confrontation. Win/Loose. Conquest spirit. Constant change of strategies.
        
So, some of these individuals appeal to their animal nature which has remained dormant until then:

  • These people under a shortage situation usually try to alter the situations that produced them, eventhough they may not evaluate the consequences that this might bring.
  • That is why they usually react almost instinctively, without giving it a second thought in a purely reflex move by means of which they only wish to survive, revealing a change in their own personal traits that will permanently confront them to the world in an unhappy, inefficient way.
