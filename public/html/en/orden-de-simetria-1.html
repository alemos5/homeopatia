Simmetry Dynamics

The manifestation Phases of the different simmetries, are under no doubt, dynamic, meaning not static.

It all depends on the individual´s interaction with its environment, regarding the different circumstances and their corresponding influence.

So much so, both the 2 Phases and their 4 Determinations, may vary from one circumstance to another.

At times one may be in harmony,  then undergo an excess or a shortage, facing different situations or eventualities.

Then the Stability Phase might imply harmony, where the individual ought to be at its peak, depending on their own singularities and individual talents.

When an individual gets stuck in any of the Instability Phase Determinations, he will then start losing integral harmony.  This lack of harmony will distort his perception of reality, as if  watching through a prism, thus originating a totally different way of feeling and interacting in the world.

Symmetry order 1

Symmetry order 1 presents a symmetry degree in which:

  1. His relation to the world (Tendency): is influenced by animal information.
  2. His relation to himself, his “Heart” (Experience) is of mineral information.
  3. His intimate nature (Rescue, Restoration) is of vegetal nature.
People responding to symmetry order 1 will mainly seek to be appreciated (Vital Need) for what they are, for Their Identity (Patrimony).

Identity Stability

People responding to symmetry order 1:

  • Are always active and are easily recognized because they never rest, that their necessary condition towards the world is movement.
  • They are constantly working, surrounding themselves by newcomers who they dazzle with their knowledge, intelligence, their brand-new projects and their wit.
    They are tireless, are always seeking new goals and challenges to work with in every field, given that his main theme is space and his world desideratum in the world is expansion.
    Reluctant to follow others, more inclined to be the ones who guide the steps to follow.
  • Many people see them as role-models and they ususally transmit energy and inspire trust upon their subordinates or their family and their close circles, given their tendency: coverage of the surroundings.
  • Nevertheless, their mineral heart will make them always seek for perfection and stability (necessary condition and individual desideratum), they might turn out to be highly demanding with themselves and others, never giving up if they can prove themselves better with a new action.
  • In harmony, the challenges they may actively pose to others, will be in most cases successful as they will rely upon their own identity traits (Theme: consolidation) (Experience: stability of their individuality)

Due to this articulation: animal towards the world and mineral in the heart:

  • They will seek to be appreciated for their individuality and personality, for those assets that make them unique and make them stand out from the rest.
  • Guided by this urge, they will search for spaces within a structure: meaning, they will need that all the spaces they cover {social activities, family, affections have to be perfectly ruled by a stable structure} They will order their actions by an inner classification thus imprinting reality with their personal seal.
  • That is why all his movements will always aim at his personal consolidation.
  • That is also why they will always try expansion in the world to reach perfection as a primordial goal in their lives.
  • Therefore this tendency of coverage of the surroundings implies the stability of his identity in projecting towards the world the Non Conscious Purpose of his Vegetal Nature: manifestation of his Sensitivity Transformation.

Instability Phase
(inharmonic)

Instability by Excess

Relation to the world: Confrontation/Win/Loose/Conquest Spirit.

Relation to himself: Permanent and Excessive Control.

When a person responding to Symmetry Order 1 suffers an Instability Phase by Excess:

  • his animal characteristics are reinforced and they may turn into highly compettitive people (Confrontation/Win/Loose) searching for success at any cost (Conquest Spirit).
  • Nevertheless, their mineral heart will lead them to always try and be in command of situations, imposing a strong and excessive control, almost opressive to themselves, to circumstances and to those around him.
  • These intents will have poor results, in keeping under his wing, on a permanent basis, whatever is going on, though he will try different options and possibilities to attain this, attempting to apply pressure and confronting with his control.

Instability by Shortage

Relation to the world (Tendency):

Submission/Capitulation/Hindrance.

Relation to himself (Experience):

Anxiety by devastation.

When a person responding to Symmetry Order 1 suffers an Instability Phase by Shortage:

The Instability manifests itself by the apparition of the animal-mineral nature of the individual in shortage:

  • feeling cornered and helpless, with no energy nor initiative (hindrance). He starts to develop a certain fear and therefore decides to adopt a complete state of submission towards others. Showing a complete captulation.
  • At his darkest hour, he will feel he´s lost everything. Internally, due to his mineral condition, a state of anxiety, and devastation will pervade and take hold of him completely, increasing the impossibility to regain leadership.

When a person responding to Symmetry Order 1 suffers an Instability Phase by Shortage, the following may manifest:

Inner Nature/Restoration/Rescue:

Alteration of the environment/Circumstancial Adjustment/Justification

Thus, some of these individuals appeal to their vegetal, nature, which had remained latent uptill now:

  • facing an extreme situation, they adjust to new circumstances instead of trying to change them and they keep on changing their needs and wishes according to different difficulties that they may encounter, always inefficiently.

 

SYNOPSIS

This makes evident the instability of these determinations:

  • people responding to symmetry order 1, facing an extreme situation: at first, they will confront and control to find a way to tackle the situation or they will submit and capitulate in anxiety by devastation and when the situation really gets extreme they usually adjust circumstancially, fruitlessly appealing to justification.
