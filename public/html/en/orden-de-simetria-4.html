Simmetry Dynamics

The manifestation Phases of the different simmetries, are under no doubt, dynamic, meaning not static.

It all depends on the individual´s interaction with its environment, regarding the different circumstances and their corresponding influence.

So much so, both the 2 Phases and their 4 Determinations, may vary from one circumstance to another.

At times one may be in harmony,  then undergo an excess or a shortage, facing different situations or eventualities.

Then the Stability Phase might imply harmony, where the individual ought to be at its peak, depending on their own singularities and individual talents.

When an individual gets stuck in any of the Instability Phase Determinations, he will then start losing integral harmony.  This lack of harmony will distort his perception of reality, as if  watching through a prism, thus originating a totally different way of feeling and interacting in the world.

Symmetry Order 4

Symmetry order 4 presents a symmetry degree in which:

  1. Relation to the world (Tendency): is influenced by mineral information.
  2. Relation to himself, his Heart (Experiencie) is of animal nature.
  3. Inner Nature (Rescue, Restoration) is of vegetal nature.

People responding to Symmetry Order 4 mainly seek to be accepted by others (Vital Need)}, that is what motivates most of their acts; but, they wish be accepted through what they do: their acts and their works (Patrimony).

Novelty

People responding to symmetry order 4:

  • Possibly at first sight or in a subtle quick approach, these people won´t call your attention. They are simple, calm, stable people (Necessary condition) they do not tend to stand out from the crowd. They usually do not outshine in the groups they mingle nor bring about disagreements but are perfectly integrated (WorldDesideratum) and rarely do they get out of bounds (Theme) aiming at preserving their life-structure (Relation to the world).
  • Nevertheless a deeper chat or a keener look into their crafts, professions or hobbies will open the door to a new world which is, active (Patrimony) and constantly brewing in search of variables of expression (Necessary condition) Just as a volcanoes - during their stillness might deceive the unadverted eye, thinking they are inert while they are really keeping an intense and ever changing interior activity – they hide a rich inner universe.
  • Men and women of this deteremination have a tidy and kempt look, but are not fussy nor obsessed with their appearance. On the contrary, they are always tuned with the rest and may use the same clothes for years or buy similar ones when renewing their wardrobe.
  • They are also very firm in their opinions and it´s hard to make them change their minds. They often are obstinate in their beleifs and rarely seen loosing control. They may even be undergoing a great grief or profound happinness, without anyone noticing it in the daily interaction, given that seldom do they express, neither phisically nor verbally, their frames of mind their hopes or fears.
  • For these people, there seems to exist a sort of boundary in their roundabouts, which they cannot trespass but internally they seek for inner expansion. They will always try to expand their animal heart, always trying expand their boundaries , taking their actions a step further.
  • It is through the development of their creative activities (Theme) that they let go and expand their individuality.
  • They usually are great artists, architects and engineers and are successful in everything related to creation, be it of tangible objects or pieces of music or literary work. Often their tastes and techniques are hard to classify, because they tend to create new ways and alternatives in their work. They show versatility in these creations (Individual Desideratum) but in a quiet, non flamboyant way.
  • So,, this versatility facing changes, taking place in their environments, allows them to no longer act, in order to change circumstances but, choose to modify their interior instead and their produce, so that it adjusts to oncoming changes. That is why he is also a great novelty (Experience), which he invents when circumstances force him to.

 

Owing to this articulation: mineral to the world and animal heart:

  • They want to be accepted for their acts. Deep down, they want to spark up an admiration that has nothing to do with self-indulgence nor gloating per se; but to know that others appreciate what's been accomplished with time and effort.
  • It´s within this endeavour that they bind themselves to a certain side of reality and due to their animal hearts they tend to perform many actions, expanding reality, in their search for acceptance through their works and deeds.
  • That´s why they will try integration to the world in order to develop their versatility,as a main goal in their lives.
  • Therefore, this tendency towards invariability in the way he has structured his life, means to find his novelty in the projection to the world of the Non Conscious purpose of his Vegetal Nature: to manifest the Sensitivity of Transformation.

Instability Phase
(inharmony)

Instability by Excess

Relation to the world (Tendency): Resistance to the environment/Inclusion or Rejection.
Relation to himself (Experience): Constant change of strategies.
        
When a person responding to symmetry order 4 enters an Instability Phase by Excess:

  • some of their traits will be enhanced and frustration will prevail given that he will not be able to integrate to the environment nor to others.
  • In this case, he breeds a resistance to everything around him or may well violently reject his environment, or generate himself a forced inclusion. In both cases this is not brought about through actual acts of facts but, through different internal attitudes by means of which, he intends to obtain an artificial harmony, with no results at all.
  • He will try to win acceptance by the only way he knows, actions from his internal world. Nevertheless, his confusion will make him, not sure of himself, rapidly feel frustrated, rehearsing different and constant strategies of forced inclusion or of exclusion, with no result of either of them.
  • His animal heart, might never set his head at rest and spend all the time scheming new plans that never give shape or are left half-way.
  • This broadens the conflict between an animal heart, that keeps him actively in touch with himself and must work to get to know himself and his external attitude prone to stillness, rest and passivity.

 

Instability by Shortage

  • Relation to the world(Tendency): Contraction.
  • Relation to himself (Experience): Distress by inhibition.

Inharmonic conditions may also lead him to a Shortage situation:

  • In this case the individual isolates himself finding shelter in his inner-self. These are those contraction moments when he closes himself to others and decides to shut off from any external bond. His way of adjusting and not being out of key, is no longer by making efforts to please but, trying to pass unadverted and not making contact. Away from the look of others and not counting with their advise, they will try to make themeselves invisible, to be able to stay far away.
  • They become isolated and a prevailing strong state of inhibition and distress takes over his life. Like this inhibited they present themselves to their companions in tasks and work and also to their families, friends and beloved ones.
  • Eventhough depending on them, they continue to search for acceptance, In this determination, this person will decide to stay away from the rest, to contract in himself during the period of distress.

 

When a person responding to symmetry order 4, suffers an instability phase by Shortage , this may manifest itself:

Inner Nature/Restoration/Rescue:

Altering the environment/Circumstancial Adjustment/Justification

Thus, some of these individuals will appeal to their vegetal nature, , which has remained latent until then:

  • when he is assaulted by this total shortage condition, having reached to a limit, a compensating impulse of vegetal nature will appear. They will react adjusting circumstancially and defending themselves, altering the outer world and justifying these changes, in a fruitless way.

Synopsis 

This enforces the instability of these determinations:

  • people responding to symmetry order 4 facing an extreme situation: at first, they resist, reject or integrate to the new circumstance and look for different ways of tackling them effectively, either contracting against the world with distress and inhibition and when the situation gets really extreme, they usually try to adjust, to naturalize it altering the enviroment and justifying himself, with no results al all.
