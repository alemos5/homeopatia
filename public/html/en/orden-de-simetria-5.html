Simmetry Dynamics

The manifestation Phases of the different simmetries, are under no doubt, dynamic, meaning not static.

It all depends on the individual´s interaction with its environment, regarding the different circumstances and their corresponding influence.

So much so, both the 2 Phases and their 4 Determinations, may vary from one circumstance to another.

At times one may be in harmony,  then undergo an excess or a shortage, facing different situations or eventualities.

Then the Stability Phase might imply harmony, where the individual ought to be at its peak, depending on their own singularities and individual talents.

When an individual gets stuck in any of the Instability Phase Determinations, he will then start losing integral harmony.  This lack of harmony will distort his perception of reality, as if  watching through a prism, thus originating a totally different way of feeling and interacting in the world.

Symmetry Order 5

Symmetry order 5 presents a symmetry degree in which:

  1. Relation to the world (Tendency): is influenced by animal information.
  2. Relation to himself, his heart (Experience) is of vegetal nature.
  3. Inner Nature (Rescue, Restoration) is of mineral nature.

People responding to symmetry order 5 seek mainly to be appreciated by others (Vital Need)}, this is what moves them in most of their acts; but they wish to be appreciated for their emotions and what they are able to express emotionally (Patrimony).

People responding to symmetry order 5:

  • By nature, these people are very restless and vivacious and usually stand out wherever they may be. They are that sort of people who are possibly in movement (Necessary condition), from here to there, working actively to be the doers of their own fate and circumstances.
  • They don´t like anybody to impose rules nor conditions upon them but are willing to open their own path in the world, they expand (World Desideratum) in every space (External theme) so that others take after them for their sensitivity (Inner theme) and thus they may be happier or feel better.
  • In this way they may stand out in management, or leading at the head of companies or groups, where they are loved and respected for their effort for making everybody feel comfortable and at ease. Generally their ambition has no limits and each achievement is only a step for the coverage , of as much as possible, of his surroundings (Tendency)
  • Nevertheless, his vegetal heart will allow an adequate variability (Inner Necessary Condition) measuring the right moment for certain actions, given that they are connected emotionally (Inner theme) to the surrounding circumstances.
  • In harmony, most of their intents, to overcome situations, will succeed, given that they will know the exact and precise moment when to manifest their sensitivity (Individual Desideratum)or at least know when it´s the time to step aside to favour theiremotional preservation (Patrimony).
  • They also possess, a refined sense of taste and they usually work hard to expand their rich and unique inner world, by which they create unique objects implying on them their personal brand.

 

Due to this articulation: animal to the world and vegetal heart:

  • They will want to be appreciates for their sensitivity, which makes them unique and stand out from the rest. In relation to themselves, these people wish to pass onto posterity for thesecreations but most of all they want to be appreciated for their emotions and the way in which they take care and protect others. They do not only seek for fame and power, but also to be respected and loved for the emotional quality with which they conduct their relatives.
  • Within this urge, they will search for space from variability: meaning they will need that all the space they cover (social activities, family, relations) to be adjusted to their emotional values. If circumstances favour it, they may get to be great leaders; their actions having a great impact upon their area of influence upto an unthought level. They are emotional leaders, whose legacy may be remembered long after death.
  • That is why their movements will always look for a personal sensitive expression.
  • That´s why they will try expansion in the world to manifest their sensitivity, which is their primordial goal in life.
  • So, therefore this tendency to cover the surroundings means his intention to perpetuate himself in the projection to the world of the Non Conscious purpose of his Mineral Nature: to manifest the Perfection of Integrity.

 

Instability Phase
(inharmonic)

Instability by Excess

Relation to the world (Tendency): Confrontation, Win/Loose. Conquering Spirit.
Relation to himself (Experience): Circumstancial Adjustment. Justification.

When a person responding to symmetry order 5 enters an Instability Phase by Excess:

  • This will enhance his animalstrokes; he may turn into someone only willing to win at any cost, placing himself in a positin of permanent competition, ein places where he can only win or lose under any circumstance.
  • Nevertheless, he manages to adjust thanks to his vegetal heart and also having a good enough nose to keep on varying circumstances of struggle and conquest. The intensity of his experiences make these adjustments cicumstancial and temporary and therefore of very little use, ending up in frustration and deception.
  • In the long run, his actions at this extent of excess, depend upon a sea of feelings in which he adjust to whatever may come next, ending up in a barren and at times harmful chaos, for himself and to others around him, methodically justifying himself for his conduct.

 

Instability by Shortage

  • Relation to the world (Tendency): Submission. Surrender. Hindrance.
  • Relation to himself (Experience): Fear and hesitation.

Lack of harmony may also lead him to a Shortage condition:

  • His animal nature leaves him helpless, fearing others and in a state of complete submission , where his born leader qualities have disappeared and are left buried, abandoning him to the mercy of others.
  • His sensitivity has turned to fear, in a hesitating condition, that leads him to surrender, meaning that he will consciously and voluntarily hand over his affairs to others, those against which he would have fought or defied at another time.

 

When a person responding to symmetry order 5 suffers an instability phase by Shortage , this may manifest itself:

Inner Nature/Restoration/Rescue:
Resistance to the environment. Inclusion. Rejection. Constant and excessive control.

In this way, some of these individuals appeal to their mineral, nature, which has remained latent until then:

  • when total shortage takes place, his mineral, nature will appear to the rescue, pushing him to control the external world and circumstances, in such an excessive way but wrongly directed and in vain.

Synopsis

This makes evident the instability of these determinations:

  • people responding to symmetry order 5, facing an extreme situation: at first they confront, fight appealing to all the emotional variables they possess or voluntarily submit, they surrender, by fear and hesitation. When the situation turns extreme they usually try to control reality in a fruitless way.
