Simmetry Dynamics

The manifestation Phases of the different simmetries, are under no doubt, dynamic, meaning not static.

It all depends on the individual´s interaction with its environment, regarding the different circumstances and their corresponding influence.

So much so, both the 2 Phases and their 4 Determinations, may vary from one circumstance to another.

At times one may be in harmony,  then undergo an excess or a shortage, facing different situations or eventualities.

Then the Stability Phase might imply harmony, where the individual ought to be at its peak, depending on their own singularities and individual talents.

When an individual gets stuck in any of the Instability Phase Determinations, he will then start losing integral harmony.  This lack of harmony will distort his perception of reality, as if  watching through a prism, thus originating a totally different way of feeling and interacting in the world.

Symmetry Order 6

Symmetry Order 6 presents a symmetry degree in which:

  1. Relation to the world (Tendency): influenced by vegetal information.
  2. Relation to himself, his "Heart" (Experience) is of mineral nature.
  3. Inner Nature (Rescue, Restoration) is of animal nature.
People who respond to symmetry order 6, mainly seek to be loved by others (Vital Need), this is what motivates most of their actions. They wish to be loved for what they are, for their identity (Patrimony).

Identity Stability

People responding to symmetry order 6:

  • An individual of this symmetry order will tend to be closely in touch with his sensibility(Necessary Condition towards the world) and alert to the world and to those people around him. Probably susceptible to changes going on in any of the fields in which they move around – at work, family or emotional-, for example: where they are bound to be appreciated and loved for their good response (Tendency) and for their capability to change their stands and decisions adjusting (Theme in the world) on behalf of their relation to others.
  • Affectionate and attentive to others, these people may spend a long time pondering and in general are very keen on drawing conclusions about others and on moving ahead to future events. If they set their minds to it, they may reach to be excellent political or financial analysts, welfare workers or art critics.
  • Due to this ability to change along with the world around him, most of the times they are prepared for unstable situations, given the nature they make use of, they are able to keep afloat transforming reality (World Desideratum) even in the worse scenarios or most drastic movements.
  • Nevertheless, deep in their hearts, they wish to be acknowledged for their individuality and personality trying to be loved for those qualities that make them unique.
  • They will try to keep up their identity stability (Experience) resisting any external influence willing to change them. In fact their actions and works might be recognized because on them is their author´s brand, a permanent signature of their style.
  • The sensitivity with which they interrealate with the world, must work on behalf of his mineral heart, asking him to assimilate all this into his identity. This may take place when putting in order the sensitive world by an inner calssification, where they will also want to leave their brand as a witness for their sensitivity.

Due to this articulation: vegetal to the world and mineral heart:

  • they will want to be loved for their individuality and personality, those qualities that make them unique and make them stand out from the rest.
  • With this urge they will seek to adjust to circumstances from within theirstructure: meaning they will need all their actions (social activities, family, affections), to be perfectly ruled and structured. They will set their actions in order with an inner classification adjustingto reality with their personal brand.
  • This is how by means of the dependent sensitivity by which their lives are ruled, they will search for personal consolidation.
  • That is why they will try to transform the world in order to attain perfection the primordial goal of their lives.
  • That´s why this tendency for response and instability facing circumstances, makes their identity stability possible, when projecting to the world the Non Conscious Purpose of their Animal Nature: to manifest the Versatility of Expansion.

Instability Phase
(inharmonic)

Instability by Excess

Relation to the world (Tendency): Altering the environment.
Relation to himself (Experience): Permanent and excessive control.

When a person responding to symmetry order 6 suffers an Instability Phase by Excess:

  • they will keep on altering different and instable actions according to what´s going on around them. He lives in a variable atmosphere where he can easily shift from one mood to another, enclosed in a flow of feelings and experiences that might play him more than a few mishaps.
  • He will feel the need of exerting an excessive control over everything going on. Possibly he will make a great effort to be in command of the situations he finds himself in, not with a conquering spirit and blindly looking for success but as someone wanting to keep things in such a state that he may know and anticipate.
  • Rarely will he take action tending to take the lead of events he might even become a spectator longing to be the "know it all" person. This is about a fruitless effort to keep everything under his wing, trying different possibilities and options to attain this.

Instability by Shortage

Relation to the world (Tendency): Subordination. Surrender.Vulnerability.
Relation to himself (Experience): Anxiety of devastation.

When a person responding to symmetry order 6 suffers an Instability Phase by Shortage:

Instability will manifest itself as it shows in an individual of a vegetal-mineral nature, in shortage:

  • Lack of harmony by shortage, will take away any possibility of taking hold of a situation and will a affect his entire sensitivity. He may even present a subordinate condition to circumstances with an absolute and deep feeling of surrender leaving him completely vulnerable to others.
  • It´s s sort of anxiety together with a profound feeling of rupture, a point of no return (anxiety of devastation) that overwhelms him, leaving him totally subordinate, defeated and prone to give it all up, feeling he can´t hold on anymore.

When a person responding to symmetry order 6 suffers an instability phase by shortage, this may become manifest:

Inner Nature/restoration/rescue:

Confrontation. Win/Loose. Conquering Spirit/Change of strategies

In this way, some of these individuals appeal to their animal, nature, that had remained latent until then:

  • Facing an extreme situation and amidst this scenery, possibly his "animality"resting in his inner nature will come to the rescue, making him undergo numerous, fast, unexpected actions but, at the same time inefficient, surprising everybody with an ability to quarrel, to fight back and an unique resistance. Nevertheless this rescue mission, will rarely prove effective and the energy required to bring about this restoration is almost always never enough.

Synopsis

This confirms the instability of these determinations:

  • people responding to symmetry order 6 facing an extreme situation: at first they will alter the environment appealing to excessive control of all the varying emotions they possess or vulnerable they will subordinate and surrender. Facing the new situation they find themselves in a condition where anxiety of devastationwill prevail. When the situation turns extreme they usually try to confront reality trying to conquer it appealing to a permanent change of strategies in a fruitless way.
