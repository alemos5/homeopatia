As we may see in the following chart, in Symmetry Order 3, relation to the world and to himself are shared by mineral and vegetal realms.  Whoever may possess this symmetry, might behave as symmetry 6 or 8.  Therefore it´s the clinical study that will determine to which of these two he belongs; plus the corresponding interrogation.
